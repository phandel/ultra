Training Regimen & Structure

  • Therapy Preventative Chiropractic: Weekly structural audits focusing on pelvic alignment, hip flexor mobility, and ankle/foot mechanics.

Biomechanics & Pacing

  • Cadence Shift: Consciously working to increase from 150 SPM to 160+ SPM to eliminate overstriding and braking forces on pavement. Hit 161 SPM successfully on 5/11.
  • Interval Strategy: Transitioning from continuous running to a strict 4-minute run / 1-minute walk interval to preserve leg muscles and practice "power hiking" transitions.
  • Heart Rate Zones: Capable of passing the nasal breathing test into the 140s due to high-incline base. Will execute Joe Friel 30-min time trial to lock in precise LTHR zones.

Nutrition Strategy

  • Deficit Math: Targeting 1 lb/week loss. Formula: 2,300 Base Calories + Run Calories - 500 Cal Deficit.
  • Recovery Fueling: Emphasis on complex carbs (oatmeal for glycogen replenishment) and lean protein (rotisserie chicken breast) post-long run.
  • Intra-Run: Actively fueling and hydrating (water + calories) during outdoor efforts to prevent thick blood, cramping, and cardiac drift.
