Here's the plan

To mark turning 50, I'm running 50 miles in a single day - fifty 1-mile loops around my neighborhood, from early morning until evening.

You don't need to be a runner to join in. Come run or walk a lap with me, stop by to cheer, or just show up for the Oktoberfest at 6 PM. Every lap is better with company, so join me for any part of the day that works for you.

Let me know you're coming below - hope you can make it!

The route

The whole day runs on a simple 1-mile loop right around the neighborhood - a 0.75-mile outer loop plus a 0.25-mile inner loop, both passing my driveway at 1136 Crescent Dr. Come find me there anytime - I roll past about every 15 minutes.
